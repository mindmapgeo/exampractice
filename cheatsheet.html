🧭 The Ultimate Call Algorithm
Every protocol, in the order you'll actually hit them on a live call. Follow the arrows.
📞 Call Connects
Blue / black screen?
Wait 60 sec → "Hello, this is your Ukrainian interpreter. Is there anybody on the line?" → still nothing → apologize and disconnect.
↓
👋 Greeting MANDATORY · SCRIPTED
To provider: your name → language → ID# → "everything will be interpreted"
To patient (Ukrainian): your name → target language → confidentiality → "everything interpreted"
↓
🔁 During the Call — interpret in 1st person. Watch for:
If someone talks to YOU instead of each other
👤 3rd Person
  1. Redirect them to speak to each other
  2. Exceptions: kids ≤7, emergencies, confused/dementia/trauma, mentally ill
  3. If they persist anyway — keep interpreting 1st person, log why
If you need to clarify / repeat / verify
🔔 Intervention
  1. ID yourself to the waiting party first
  2. ID yourself to the other party, ask your question
  3. Resume interpreting
If audio/video quality is bad
🔧 Troubleshoot
  1. Reposition device / toggle camera
  2. Still bad → camera off, keep going
  3. Still bad → offer new interpreter + 1-866-449-4428
If you need to step away
⏸️ Hold
  1. 10 min standard · 20 min if unspecified · indefinite if life/death
  2. Tell both parties, camera off + mute
  3. Log it, report on Discord
If asked for a gift / your hours / sight translation
🚫 Say No
  1. Be gracious
  2. Offer 2-3 alternatives
  3. Give a reason (company policy)
If provider speaks Ukrainian / patient speaks English
👁️ Shadowing
  1. Tell both parties you're staying on, listening
  2. Stay alert, keep taking notes
  3. Jump in only if something important needs interpreting
If you need to place an outbound call
📲 Dial-Out
  1. Number to dial? Who's calling?
  2. Patient's name?
  3. Leave voicemail? Callback #?
If ASL + another spoken language both needed
👥 Dual Interpretation
  1. First one connected adds the second interpreter
  2. Brief provider on hold, brief your team
  3. ASL interpreter leads
If the call is running long
⏰ Long Call
  1. 90 min → self-monitor, consider transfer
  2. 120 min → expected to transfer (unless not tired)
  3. "End of shift" alone is NOT a valid reason
↓
🏁 Wrapping Up
Closing MANDATORY · SCRIPTED
📞 Audio: offer more help → state your ID# → bye to doctor → bye to patient (Ukrainian)
📹 Video: offer more help → remind them to rate the call → bye to doctor → bye to patient (Ukrainian)
↓
📝 Call Notes ALWAYS
Leave a note every single call, even if nothing happened. Names in CAPS. Refusals → initials, or N/A in caps if none given.

"Добрий день, мене звати ___, я ваш перекладач англійської мови. Усе під час цієї сесії буде перекладатися та залишатиметься конфіденційним."

  1. Offer additional assistance (MANDATORY)
  2. State Interpreter's ID #
  3. Say goodbye to doctor
  4. Say goodbye to LEP (in Ukrainian)
📹 Closing — Video (4 steps)
  1. Offer additional assistance (MANDATORY)
  2. Remind doctor to rate the call
  3. Say goodbye to doctor
  4. Say goodbye to LEP (in Ukrainian)
⏸️ Hold — 5 Steps
  1. Inform provider of 10-min policy
  2. Inform LEP of the hold
  3. Turn camera off + mute audio
  4. Note in Call Notes
  5. Report on Discord
📲 Dial-Out — 5 Questions
  1. What phone number to dial?
  2. Who should I say is calling?
  3. Name of person/patient?
  4. Leave a voicemail message?
  5. Callback number?
🔧 Troubleshoot — 3 Steps
  1. Ask provider to reposition / toggle camera
  2. If unresolved: camera off, continue
  3. If still unresolved: connect to another interpreter + give Tech Support # (1-866-449-4428)
🔔 Intervention — 3 Steps
  1. Identify self as interpreter to waiting party
  2. Identify self to the other party, request clarification
  3. Resume interpreting
Script: "This is the interpreter speaking / Excuse me, the interpreter needs to clarify..."
👤 3rd Person Exceptions
3rd person is ALLOWED for:
  • • Children ≤ 7 years old
  • • Emergencies (summarizing)
  • • Dementia / substance abuse / post-surgery
  • • Mentally ill patients
  • • Trauma-triggered during call
📝 Call Notes Rules
  • • ALWAYS leave a note, even if nothing happened
  • • Names → CAPITAL LETTERS
  • • Refused initials → type initials given
  • • Full refusal → type N/A in caps
  • • Note reason for refusal
🚫 "Say No" Model
  1. Be gracious
  2. Offer 2-3 alternatives
  3. Give a reason (e.g., company policy)
Use for: gift offers, working hours questions, sight translation requests
